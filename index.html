About Us

We're making healthy eating actually easy.

Snaplate is an Android app built by a first-year CS student at SOIT RGPV, Bhopal. The idea was simple — counting calories shouldn't require a nutrition degree or manual data entry. So we built an AI that does it for you, instantly.

🎯 Our Mission

To make food awareness accessible to everyone — students, busy professionals, fitness beginners — by removing all friction from calorie tracking.

👁️ Our Vision

A world where understanding what you eat takes a single snap — no labels, no barcodes, no effort.

The Problem

Eating healthy is hard when you don't know what's on your plate.

People want to eat better — but existing tools make it tedious, frustrating, and easy to quit.

😩

Manual logging is a nightmare

Apps like MyFitnessPal require searching hundreds of entries, estimating portions, and repeating this for every meal — every single day.

🏷️

No label? No data.

Homemade food, restaurant meals, and street food have no barcode. Most apps give up — leaving people completely guessing.

📉

People give up quickly

80% of people who start calorie tracking quit within two weeks — because it's just too tedious to maintain.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Who faces this problem?

🧑‍🎓 College students eating hostel / canteen food
💼 Working professionals with no time to track
🏋️ Fitness beginners starting their health journey
👨‍👩‍👧 Parents managing family nutrition
🩺 People managing diet-related health conditions
Our Solution

Point. Snap. Know.

Snaplate replaces every tedious step with a single camera tap. No searching, no typing, no guessing.

How it works

01

Open and point your camera

Launch Snaplate and aim at any food — a plate, a snack, a bowl. No special setup needed.

02

Gemini AI identifies it instantly

Google's Gemini Vision model analyses the image, recognises the food, and retrieves accurate nutritional data.

03

See your full nutrition breakdown

Calories, protein, carbs, fat — displayed clearly and automatically logged to your daily tracker.

How it helps you

✅ No manual data entry — snap and it's logged automatically.
✅ Works on any food — home-cooked, restaurant, or street food.
✅ Daily calorie tracker — know where you stand every day.
✅ Fast and private — results in 3 seconds, no account needed to start.
